  • Part Time Production Staff at Muslimah Wear Boutique

    • I was 18 when I first applied for my first ever job at this one Muslimah Wear Boutique. I still remember I was so excited because it will be my first ever experience entitled as a worker after graduated from high school...and yeah, you can say that I'm kinda excited to earn my own money after all ^3^ Well, I was nervous too since I don't have my family and friends with me but the other production staff were so kind to me since the very first day! :D Anyway, I think I worked for almost 1 year and then I stopped since online classes has already started and I need to focus ^^;


  • Part Timer at Boost Juice

    • First thing first,this is literally my favourite part time job ever!!! I applied for this position in February 2022 and I got accepted straight away! I still remember I was nervous because I had an online interview but good things that I did well! Long story short, I then signed some agreement stuff first, got a typhoid injection since I will working in f&b industry then I got my uniform, apron, and a nametag! It was so cool! I met many wonderful people there and I had a really kind manager too ♡♡♡ My other co-worker are nice and kind and friendly so I don't have a hard time working there! No job are easy in this world! and of course working in f&b industry is tiring and exhausting but it's all worth it because I mostly had a really fun time working there! After all, I got free meal (they give you one drink!) everyday so it feels like a little treat for myself after a long tiring day! Moreover, I learnt a lot of things here! I succesfully memorized all sort of drinks, I learnt how to make many drinks at once (phew!), I learnt how to deal with customers, and manyyy more! I could say that working here is fun because of the environment too! Ohh the most important thing is, I get to try almost everything on the menu here for free! hehe! ^3^
